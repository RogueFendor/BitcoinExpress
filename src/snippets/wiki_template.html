About

With the increasing demand and popularity of electronic currencies based on Blockchain technologies such as:
  • Bitcoin
    • >
  • Dash
  • Dogecoin
  • Litecoin
  • Ethereum
End users have been made aware and exposed to these new technologies and means that provide functionality to conduct transactions in a decentralized manner. BitcoinExpress is a design exploration targeted for Android devices. The aim of this project is to provide a fully functional Bitcoin Wallet for Android Devices but apart from the usual functionality _i.e send and receive transactions_, the target of this project is to provide additionally: integrated "Static" and "Real time" circular web based data exchange mechanisms. Which means BitcoinExpress acts a **client server** and **server client** platform allowing
  • customer to service provider
  • service provider to customer
    • communications! This will allow the wallet to act as a client for a communications and information platform that encapsulates key aspects for a crypto currency based "_community infrastructure_" such as:
    • Geolocation information
    • service subscriptions
    • service publications
    • Instant Messaging
    In a Nutshell! BitcoinExpress is part of an Android bitcoin electronic wallet application for individuals who whish to join, increase and create a safe and secure community infrastructure for bitcoin users! But BitcoinExpress is not your conventional website. That means not anyone can browse this site, BitcoinExpress is designed to act effectively as a broker for users who have the BitcoinExpressWallet installed on their Android devices. Your BitcoinExpress broker is generating on request a list of available bitcoin user locations and/or establishments from its internal Database and distributes these to BitcoinExpressWallets that are continously listening for changes and updates and the incoming data on a local database. Your BitcoinExpressWallet allows you to update,upgrade and create new entries about establishments that accept bitcoin worldwide. Your BitcoinExpressWallet is be able to capture your currrent location via your devices GPS features and map according your configuration a list of establishments and service providers that accept Bitcoin. For example "nearby" would generate a list of service providers that accept Bitcoin in your current area. Your BitcoinExpressWallet enables You to: subscribe AND/OR publish That means you can broadcast and/or listen to a list of topics available on your device. In addition you may create custom topics for a selected set of users which can very useful for creating isloated chat channels among other users. Once yoy have subscribed or published to a service and/or service request you will notified on incoming notifications through the Androids "Push Notification" features. Example: Assume you have subscribed to a custom topic that deals with the review of a bitcoin to euro exchange service and other BitcoinExpressWallet users are encouraged to provide a short review about their experience with this specific service. Now whenever another BitcoinExpressWallet user lets say Adam from India publishes his experience to your custom topic your BitcoinExpressWallet will alert you immmediately of an incoming message, and you can read what Adam has to say about the Exchange Service!
